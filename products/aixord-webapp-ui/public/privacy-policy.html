Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 25, 2026

PMERIT Technologies LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the AIXORD platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Account Information: Email address and name when you create an account
  • API Keys: Generated authentication tokens for platform access
  • Project Data: Project names, objectives, and governance state you create within the platform
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our services

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Send technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Protect against fraudulent or illegal activity

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent or at your direction
  • With service providers who assist in our operations
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure storage of API keys using industry-standard hashing
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Data portability

7. Third-Party Services

Our platform may integrate with third-party AI providers (such as Anthropic, OpenAI, Google). When you use these integrations, your prompts and data may be processed by these providers according to their respective privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the effective date.
