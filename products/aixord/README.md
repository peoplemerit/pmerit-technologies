## Product Summary

**AIXORD (AI Execution Order Framework)** is a methodology and template pack for developers who use AI assistants and need:

- **Authority:** Structured execution orders from AI to human
- **Execution:** Sequential, single-task workflows with confirmation gates
- **Confirmation:** Evidence-based completion before proceeding

Inspired by military OPORD (Operations Order) doctrine, adapted for AI-human collaboration.

## What AIXORD Solves

| Problem | AIXORD Solution |
|---------|-----------------|
| AI context limits | Living documents with handoffs |
| Specification drift | Reality-first audits before specs |

| Role confusion | Clear AI → Human directionality |
