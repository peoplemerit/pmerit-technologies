## Quick Start

1. Open your AI chatbot (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, or any other)
2. Choose the variant that matches your platform
3. Copy the entire contents of that file
4. Paste as your first message
5. The AI will acknowledge AIXORD governance
6. Start working with clear authority and structure
