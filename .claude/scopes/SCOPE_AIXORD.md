## Rebrand Rationale

The product evolved from "Scope Order System" to "AIXORD" to align with:

1. **Platform Evolution:** PMERIT platform governance evolved AADOS → AIXORD
2. **Clearer Identity:** "AIXORD" is a distinct, memorable name vs generic "Scope Order"
3. **Doctrine Focus:** Emphasizes the military OPORD-inspired structure
4. **Authority:** "Execution Order" conveys directive nature, not suggestion
