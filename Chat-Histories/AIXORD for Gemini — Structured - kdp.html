Book Description

Stop fighting your AI. Start collaborating with it.

AIXORD (AI Execution Order) is a governance framework that transforms chaotic Gemini conversations into structured, productive projects. If you've ever lost hours to forgotten context, scope creep, or AI doing things you didn't ask for — this book is for you.

WHAT YOU'LL LEARN:

  • The Authority Model — You are the Director. AI is your Architect and Commander. Clear roles eliminate confusion.
  • The Two Kingdoms — Separate planning (Ideation) from building (Realization) with a mandatory gate between them.
  • The DAG System — Map dependencies so nothing gets built before its prerequisites are complete.
  • 7 Quality Dimensions — Evaluate every decision against Best Practices, Completeness, Accuracy, Sustainability, Reliability, User-Friendliness, and Accessibility.
  • Scope Reassessment — When plans fail, gracefully pivot without losing your progress.
  • Session Continuity — Never lose context between conversations again with the HANDOFF protocol.

WHAT'S INSIDE:

  • 15 chapters covering everything from setup to advanced usage
  • Gemini-specific tips for both Advanced (Gems) and Free tiers
  • Real-world examples showing AIXORD in action
  • Quick Reference Card for instant command lookup
  • Troubleshooting FAQ for common issues

WHO THIS IS FOR:

  • Developers using Gemini for coding projects
  • Entrepreneurs building products with AI assistance
  • Content creators managing complex workflows
  • Anyone frustrated with unstructured AI conversations
