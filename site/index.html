About PMERIT Technologies

PMERIT Technologies LLC is the for-profit product arm of the PMERIT ecosystem. We build software that solves real problems — AI governance for developers, pantry management for families, and educational content for curious minds.

Every product is built by a solo developer using AI-augmented workflows, governed by the AIXORD framework, and deployed on Cloudflare's global edge network. Our sister entity, PMERIT Foundation, is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit providing free education technology.
